#Collections

#Bags&Beyond

#TheClassics

#TheClassics is our everyday range crafted of soft savage , nappa & naturally dyed & milled leather produced from cattle hides & skins! It is full grained leather typically dyed and tanned in our adept tannery unit , keeping its soft hand touch.

#TheDistressed

Our #Distressed leather is washed & lightly tumbled with paraffin waxes to create a well-worn, comfortable look which gets better as it ages, giving each bag a unique look and beautiful variations in color and shading. Distressed is easily recognizable by its appearance alone. A specially formulated mixture of oil , waxes & creams is used to give them their final touch of a high gloss surface with very light, transparent finish. These soft, full bodied hides offer depth of color and a lightly worn appearance, the #Rugged look!

#TheVintage

#Vintage leather also labelled as #oiled, #waxed or #pullup leather is robust smooth leather with two tones and the lustrous look which defines this range predominantly.Due to its soft and scratch-sensitive finish, the leather quickly gets a vintage-patina, which is a desired effect. The patina arises by folding, scratching and stretching.

#Vintage is our forever prevailing #timeless range!

#ThePremium

The #Premium is our #Luxury range made of full grain high quality leather effectively tumbled & oiled/waxed with a smooth finish and a signature sheen. The minimal two-tonish effect setting the background of a highly luxurious leather enhances its royalty with a feel good touch!

#TheWallets

#TheWallets is the #must have range which includes,#ladies wallets, #men's wallets , #notecase , #keyrings & #charms in a wide variety of leather. Interestingly we are an eminent wallet supplier to Italy , Spain ,Czech Republic & Netherland. We develop the basics with an edge!!
